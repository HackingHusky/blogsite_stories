My Next Passion Project

While studying for the OSCP/PNPT, I had a huge thought. IT certifications, learning, and hearing about the skills gap really got me interested in finding an answer to this problem.

You can see the pattern here… The issue with these posts is that we are relying on AI to feel the gaps, cyber isn't considered "entry", and honestly, it feels like it's a gatekeeper position, but that is the huge opposite here. I think there is an extreme amount of noise here.

Now we have Hack the Box, Try Hack Me, Hack Smarter, OffSec, etc., which are all great (except I am biased toward Try Hack Me, and this is what really got me thinking about making this). Training platforms should be able to bridge the gaps and what employers are looking for. AI shouldn’t take the training data and make an AI model try to replace them *cough* *try hack is me* *cough* (I made an article on that one).

BueduEducation shouldn’t be extremely pricey either. Now Hack the Box and Hack Smarter are great. Also, I was thinking back when I was 20–25, learning IT was great from my best friend, and it got me into IT. However, living paycheck to paycheck was rough. We've all been there, where the common meal was microwave burritos/ramen, at least for me. So, trying to afford the price of boxes on Hack the Box can get pricy. I love how Hack Smarter is extremely affordable. Especially pay what you can.

Going back to experiences, though, like with my first job as a dishwasher at a brew pub and college. I was looking for additional education I could access while learning my craft. That’s what brought me to TryHackMe when I was just starting. I wanted to get better, which helped me with my finals. That also gave me the driving force to do this project.

We can all agree that, right now, changing that is almost impossible. I want to change that. If someone needs to gain the skills to fill that gap, then let’s make that change. I've seen that a lot on LinkedIn, and I want to help make that change. That’s why I wanted to build the platform. It’s still in testing phases and working on course releases.

This is a basic layout for now, but I want to do more than just offensive security. I plan to create an IT learning platform covering System Administration, Ethical Hacker, and Basic coding in Python/C#.

I was really cleaning out my library this weekend, and wanted to give classes on the important ones. I want to make this my passion project because I believe that if anyone wants to make a career change and take the next step, I'd rather be the force for change than just hear all the noise.

Will this project get people hired? No. Have to make that disclaimer now. I do want to help bridge the gaps where people are having issues. Take what I know and help others get to the next level.

During testing:

I created this tool with AI’s help using Base44. I do like the ability to edit databases, and it's in JavaScript that the owner can control the back end, too. This will take a minute to build out what I want it to look like. I don’t have a domain for it yet because I need to get the classes working first, but when I do, I will celebrate its launch day.

I prefer to build a tool that helps everyone. This is going to take some time to build out. My goal at the end of the month is to get three classes ready to go. With each section containing videos, articles, and quizzes, you can gain a certificate of completion. There won’t really be certs to earn like the SEC+ or PNPT. But everyone will be able to get the pdf and share:

However, everyone's journey is different. So I want to build and give back to the community. I don’t know everyone’s situation, but I want to make it clear that there’s a skill gap or that AI will replace the workforce. I want to be different with this site. Not going to collect any data, your learning is your learning. I'm happy to teach and strengthen the IT community. That’s all that should be about. I may stumble; it's going to be a learning process. I want it to be available for all. That’s why it's going to be free, plus I am making this with AI. Money shouldn’t rule you, but education should also be available. I’m excited and nervous about it.

I want to share this because it’s my year project, and I hope we can make a significant change.
