Passing the PNPT

Man, this has to be my wildest journey I've ever experienced. On the weekend of my retake of the OSCP, sadly, I was 10 points short of passing. With an extra day off, I took the PNPT to test my methodology. Licking my wounds up and getting back on my PC the very next day, I got in, and I completely shocked myself.

I was able to fully compromise the domain in the lab very quickly. So what was different? Well, not much really. The methodology was the same. The attack vectors are a bit different. So this told me two things. The work I put into my website, sharing notes, and the love I received for these blogs show I'm ready for the exam.

With the PNPT, you not only have to provide a report, but also a presentation to a client. Which I was comfortable doing. With the OSCP, you have to show the flag and the technical steps that led you there. Both tests have their own thought processes. There will be times when you run into a brick wall and need to figure it out.
